Our Story

Founded in 2000, Omega Medical Billing has been dedicated to helping healthcare providers optimize their revenue cycle management. With over two decade of experience in medical billing, our team of certified specialists combines industry expertise with cutting-edge technology to deliver exceptional results for practices of all sizes and specialties. We understand the complexities of the healthcare landscape and are committed to providing personalized, compliant, and efficient billing solutions that enhance financial performance while ensuring patient satisfaction.

Medical Billing Services

Quality First

  • Omega Medical Billing is one of the top medical billing companies in the USA, specializing in managing the revenue cycle for practices. We provide access to experienced staff, including R&D resources and platform-independent professionals. With our advanced expertise in medical billing, practice management, audit review, and healthcare management consulting, we are proud to offer quality-first services to medical billing providers across multiple specialties. Our skilled billing specialists manage the entire medical billing cycle to boost collections, reduce denial rates, and lower administrative costs.
  • By partnering with us, you can trust in the strength of our medical billing and practice management services while focusing on the well-being of your patients. Our customized solutions ensure full compliance with state laws and federal regulations, including CMS standards, to protect patients while maximizing efficiency and revenue.

HIPAA Compliance

Our Commitment to Protecting Patient Privacy

At Omega Medical Billing, safeguarding patient privacy and upholding the highest standards of confidentiality has been a cornerstone of our operations since our establishment in 2000. As a trusted name in medical billing for over two decades, we fully understand the critical importance of compliance with the Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act (HIPAA). Our team stays current with evolving regulations and industry best practices, providing our clients with peace of mind knowing that their data—and their patients’ data—is in safe hands. At Omega, HIPAA compliance isn’t just a policy; it’s part of our culture and a reflection of the trust we've built with healthcare providers across the nation. Your confidentiality is our priority. Your compliance is our commitment.
