Our Process

Billing Audit Process

Our comprehensive audit approach ensures thorough assessment and actionable improvement recommendations.

Initial Discovery & Data Collection

We begin with a thorough discovery of your current billing practices and processes. During this phase, we:

  • Review current billing policies and procedures
  • Collect data on charge capture methods
  • Evaluate documentation practices and templates
  • Gather information on billing system configurations
  • Assess current reporting capabilities and metrics

Comprehensive Process Assessment

We conduct a detailed assessment of your entire revenue cycle process. Our assessment includes:

  • Evaluation of documentation quality and completeness
  • Review of coding accuracy and compliance
  • Analysis of charge capture effectiveness
  • Assessment of claim submission workflow
  • Evaluation of denial management processes

Data Analysis & Gap Identification

We analyze all collected data to identify patterns, trends, and opportunities for improvement. Our analysis includes:

  • Financial impact assessment of identified issues
  • Root cause analysis of recurring problems
  • Benchmark comparison against industry standards
  • Compliance risk evaluation
  • Prioritization of findings based on impact and resolution effort

Comprehensive Findings & Recommendations

We deliver detailed reports with actionable recommendations for improvement. Our reporting includes:

  • Executive summary of key findings
  • Detailed assessment results by functional area
  • Quantification of financial improvement opportunities
  • Prioritized recommendations with implementation timelines
  • Best practice guidelines for each area of improvement

Implementation Support & Follow-up

We don't just provide recommendations; we help you implement them. Our implementation support includes:

  • Development of detailed action plans
  • Staff training and education on best practices
  • Process redesign assistance
  • Policy and procedure development
  • Follow-up assessments to measure improvement
