Our Process

Medical Billing Process

Our comprehensive approach ensures maximum revenue and minimal administrative burden for your practice.

Patient Registration & Insurance Verification

We verify insurance eligibility and benefits to ensure accurate information before services are rendered. Our thorough verification process includes:

  • Comprehensive patient demographic collection
  • Real-time insurance eligibility verification
  • Benefits and coverage confirmation
  • Patient responsibility estimation
  • Prior authorization management when required

Charge Entry & Coding

Our certified coders review documentation to ensure accurate code assignment for maximum reimbursement. Our coding expertise includes:

  • Certified professional coders specializing in multiple specialties
  • CPT, HCPCS, and ICD-10 code validation
  • Diagnosis and procedure code linkage verification
  • Documentation review for code optimization
  • Regular coding audits to maintain accuracy

Claim Submission

We submit clean claims electronically to insurance carriers within 24-48 hours of service. Our claim submission process includes:

  • Comprehensive claim scrubbing to identify and fix errors
  • Electronic submission to all major payers
  • Clearinghouse rejection management
  • Real-time claim tracking and status monitoring
  • Secondary and tertiary insurance billing

Payment Posting & Reconciliation

We post payments accurately and reconcile EOBs/ERAs to ensure proper reimbursement. Our payment management includes:

  • Daily electronic remittance advice (ERA) processing
  • Manual EOB entry for non-electronic payments
  • Payment reconciliation to ensure accurate reimbursement
  • Patient responsibility calculations
  • Deposit verification and reconciliation

Denial Management & Appeals

We aggressively work denied claims and file appeals when necessary to maximize collections. Our denial management approach includes:

  • Root cause analysis for all denied claims
  • Immediate correction and resubmission of claims
  • Strategic appeal writing with supporting documentation
  • Payer-specific appeal strategies
  • Tracking of appeal outcomes and success rates

Reporting & Analysis

We provide detailed reports on practice performance, collection rates, and areas for improvement. Our reporting includes:

  • Monthly financial performance summaries
  • Key performance indicator tracking
  • Aging accounts receivable analysis
  • Denial trend identification and resolution
  • Revenue cycle optimization recommendations
