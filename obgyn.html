OBGYN Billing Process

  • Pre-authorization for gynecological surgical procedures
  • Preventive service coverage confirmation

Specialized OBGYN Coding

Our OBGYN coding specialists ensure accurate code assignment for all women's health services:

  • Precise coding for global maternity care packages
  • Expert coding for gynecological surgeries (hysterectomies, laparoscopies)
  • Accurate preventive service and screening procedure coding
  • Proper modifiers for multiple procedures and partial maternity care
  • Compliant documentation support for complex obstetric cases

Clean Claim Submission

We achieve a 98% clean claim rate for OBGYN services through our thorough process:

  • OBGYN-specific claim scrubbing to identify coding errors
  • Verification of medical necessity documentation for procedures
  • Proper timing of global package claims submission
  • Accurate diagnosis-procedure code matching
  • Electronic submission to all major payers within 24-48 hours

frequently asked questions

OBGYN Billing FAQs

Get answers to common questions about our obstetrics and gynecology medical billing services, coding challenges, and revenue optimization strategies.

Our approach to global maternity package billing includes tracking all prenatal visits, proper timing of claim submission, coordination of benefits for changing insurance during pregnancy, and accurate coding for delivery methods. We also ensure proper handling of high-risk pregnancies that may require additional services beyond the global package, and manage partial package billing when patients transfer care.

We specialize in gynecological surgery billing, including hysterectomies, laparoscopies, and other procedures. Our approach includes verifying pre-authorizations, proper coding based on approach (open, laparoscopic, vaginal), accurate modifier usage for multiple procedures, and thorough documentation review. We ensure compliance with payer-specific guidelines while maximizing appropriate reimbursement.

Our well-woman preventive service billing includes proper coding for annual examinations, preventive screenings (mammograms, Pap tests), and ensuring correct diagnosis codes to support preventive vs. problem-oriented services. We stay current with changing preventive service guidelines across insurers, especially for services mandated as no-cost sharing under the Affordable Care Act.

We have specialized expertise in fertility treatment billing, including IUI, IVF, and related procedures. Our team verifies insurance coverage for infertility services (which varies widely), ensures proper coding for diagnostic vs. treatment services, manages cycle-based billing, and helps implement effective patient financial counseling for non-covered services. We also coordinate specialty pharmacy billing for fertility medications.
