Our Process

Oncology Billing Process

Our comprehensive approach ensures maximum revenue and minimal administrative burden for your oncology practice.

Patient Registration & Insurance Verification

We verify oncology-specific insurance benefits and coverage for urological procedures, tests, and devices before services are rendered, including:

  • Verification of coverage for diagnostic urological tests (cystoscopies, urodynamics)
  • Pre-authorization for urological procedures and interventions
  • Coverage confirmation for implantable urological devices
  • Patient responsibility estimation for high-cost urological procedures
  • Coordination of benefits for post-operative urological care

Specialized Oncology Coding

Our oncology coding specialists ensure accurate code assignment for all urological services:

  • Precise coding for diagnostic oncology services (cystoscopies, urodynamics, imaging)
  • Expert coding for surgical urological procedures (prostatectomies, nephrectomies)
  • Accurate device coding (stents, slings, prosthetics)
  • Proper modifiers for multiple urological procedures
  • Compliant documentation support for complex urological procedures

Clean Claim Submission

We achieve a 98% clean claim rate for oncology services through our thorough process:

  • Oncology-specific claim scrubbing to identify coding errors
  • Verification of medical necessity documentation for urological procedures
  • Inclusion of required diagnostic test results
  • Proper sequencing of urological diagnosis and procedure codes
  • Electronic submission to all major payers within 24-48 hours

  • Documentation support for complex urological intervention appeals
  • Appeals for denied device implantation procedures
  • Monitoring and addressing oncology-specific payer policy changes

frequently asked questions

Oncology Billing FAQs

Get answers to common questions about our oncology medical billing services, coding challenges, and revenue optimization strategies.

Common oncology claim denials include medical necessity issues for diagnostic tests, incorrect bundling of services, missing documentation for urological devices, improper use of modifiers, and lack of prior authorization for certain procedures. Our specialized denial management team addresses these issues proactively to minimize denials.

Our approach to complex urological surgery billing involves comprehensive documentation review, verification of procedure details, proper coding of the primary and secondary procedures, and ensuring all required elements for reimbursement are captured. We also manage concurrent procedures and maintain accurate surgical tracking.

Our dedicated oncology coding specialists participate in ongoing education, specialty-specific training, and oncology coding certifications. We monitor updates from the American Urological Association, attend oncology coding webinars, and maintain relationships with oncology specialty organizations to stay ahead of changes.

For urodynamic studies, we implement a specialized review process that includes verification of the specific tests performed, proper sequencing of codes, and ensuring proper documentation of medical necessity. Our team understands the complex rules around urodynamic study coding, including proper use of modifiers and correct component coding.
