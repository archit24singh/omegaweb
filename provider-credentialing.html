Provider Credentialing

Streamlined credentialing services to ensure your providers are properly enrolled with payers, minimizing administrative burden and accelerating your revenue cycle.

Overview

Comprehensive Credentialing

Provider credentialing is a critical but often time-consuming and complex process that can significantly impact your practice's revenue cycle. At Omega Medical Billing, we handle the entire credentialing lifecycle, from initial applications to ongoing maintenance and renewals.

Our team of credentialing specialists has extensive experience working with all major commercial payers, Medicare, Medicaid, and other government programs. We understand the unique requirements of each payer and ensure that all applications are complete, accurate, and submitted on time.

Our Services

Key Features

  • Complete provider enrollment with all insurance payers
  • CAQH profile creation and management
  • Medicare and Medicaid enrollment
  • Hospital privileging assistance
  • Regular status updates and tracking
  • Expiration monitoring and renewal management
  • Provider demographic updates across all payers
  • Credentialing database maintenance

Our Process

Provider Credentialing Process

Our streamlined approach ensures timely enrollment with all payers while minimizing the administrative burden for your practice.

Initial Document Collection

We begin by collecting all necessary documentation to support your providers' credentialing applications. Our comprehensive approach includes:

  • Provider information questionnaire completion
  • License verification and documentation collection
  • Education and training verification
  • Malpractice insurance documentation
  • Board certification verification

Application Submission

We prepare and submit complete, accurate applications to all required payers. Our application process includes:

  • CAQH profile creation and attestation
  • Insurance payer application completion
  • Medicare and Medicaid enrollment
  • Hospital privileging application assistance
  • Electronic submission and tracking of all applications

Follow-up With Payers

We actively follow up with payers to ensure timely processing of all applications. Our follow-up process includes:

  • Regular check-ins with payer credentialing departments
  • Prompt response to additional information requests
  • Escalation procedures for delayed applications
  • Documentation of all communications and status updates
  • Resolution of any application issues or discrepancies

Tracking & Status Updates

We provide regular updates on the status of all credentialing applications. Our tracking process includes:

  • Detailed tracking database for all applications
  • Regular status reports for your practice
  • Estimated completion timelines for each payer
  • Documentation of provider effective dates
  • Verification of provider enrollment and participation status

Ongoing Management & Renewals

We provide continuous management of provider credentials to ensure uninterrupted participation. Our ongoing management includes:

  • Expiration date tracking for all credentials
  • Proactive renewal management
  • Provider demographic updates across all payers
  • Maintenance of up-to-date CAQH profiles
  • Regular audits of credentialing information
