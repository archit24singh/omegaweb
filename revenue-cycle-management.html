Our Process

Revenue Cycle Management Process

Our comprehensive approach ensures maximum revenue, accelerated cash flow, and minimal administrative burden for your practice.

Pre-Service Financial Clearance

We manage the entire pre-service process to ensure financial clearance before appointments. Our comprehensive approach includes:

  • Appointment scheduling optimization
  • Insurance eligibility verification and benefit analysis
  • Prior authorization management
  • Patient cost estimation and financial counseling
  • Pre-registration and demographic verification

Patient Registration & Data Capture

Accurate patient data is the foundation of a clean revenue cycle. We handle:

  • Complete demographic collection (name, DOB, contact, guarantor)
  • Insurance details entry and validation
  • Consent & signature capture for treatment and billing
  • Patient portal setup for self-service updates

Coding & Charge Capture

Correct coding ensures maximum reimbursement and compliance. Our team provides:

  • Real-time charge entry from encounter notes
  • CPT/HCPCS & ICD-10 coding by certified specialists
  • Automated code scrubber to flag missing or conflicting codes
  • Modifier assignment for bundled or complex services

Claims Submission & Follow-Up

Timely, clean claims drive cash flow. We deliver:

  • Batch claim generation in payer-required format (837)
  • Electronic clearinghouse submission with built-in edits
  • Rejection/denial monitoring and rapid correction
  • Aging reports to prioritize outstanding claims

Payment Posting & Reconciliation

Accurate posting speeds up resolution and reduces patient disputes. We perform:

  • EOB/EOP import and automated payment application
  • Adjustment code mapping for write‚Äêoffs and contractual allowances
  • Patient balance calculation and statement generation
  • Bank reconciliation to confirm deposits match posted payments

Denial Management & Appeals

Recover revenue lost to denials with a structured appeals workflow:

  • Denial root-cause analysis to identify systemic issues
  • Automated denial alerts routed to specialist teams
  • Appeal letter drafting with supporting documentation
  • AR aging strategies to reduce days-in-A/R and write-offs
